Lets learn somefacts about Yo-Yo


A yo-yo is a toy consisting of an axle connected to two disks, and a string looped around the axle, similar to a spool.
It is played by holding the free end of the string known as the handle, allowing gravity to spin the yo-yo and unwind the string.
The player then allows the yo-yo to wind itself back to the player's hand, exploiting its spin. This is often called "yo-yoing".
